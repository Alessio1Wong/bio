Student Council of the SYIS

Activity Overview

The Student Council of the Shanghai Youth Science Research Institute is a student self-governance organization composed of city-level junior researchers. The council is responsible for planning, organizing, and executing various student-related activities to promote academic collaboration, innovation, and leadership.

Key Responsibilities

  • Planning and organizing student events and activities
  • Collaborating with academic and community organizations
  • Promoting innovation and scientific research among members
  • Managing communication and feedback between students and institute leaders

Duration of Service

Start Date: June 2024

End/Anticipated End Date: June 2026
