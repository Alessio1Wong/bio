School Science Lecture for Adolescents

Activity Overview

As the organizer, planner, and keynote speaker of the Student Science Forum, I developed and delivered a series of academic workshops focused on research-based learning. These sessions were attended by high school underclassmen eager to explore science and innovation, providing them with guidance and inspiration on their academic journeys.

Key Responsibilities

  • Reading Academic Papers Shared techniques for efficiently reading and analyzing academic papers to help students grasp key content.
  • Identifying Research Topics Explained methods for selecting research directions, aligning them with personal interests and academic goals.
  • Managing Research Projects Introduced strategies for planning and executing research, addressing challenges faced in long-term projects.
  • Case Studies and Applications Demonstrated the connection between theory and practice through personal research experiences and successful project examples.

Review from Attendee
